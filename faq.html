Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lingua Beats?

Lingua Beats is a platform that combines the power of music and AI to help you learn languages in a fun, interactive, and personalized way.

How do I join the waitlist?

You can join our waitlist by filling in your email in the waitlist form on our website. Once you sign up, you'll receive a confirmation email.

How are the language packs priced?

We offer a Starter Pack for 7.99€ and an Advanced Pack for 6.99€, with additional exclusive in-app content available for 2€.

Is the learning process personalized?

Yes, our system uses AI to personalize the learning experience, tailoring the content to your specific needs and progress.
